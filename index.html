Features

EVERYTHING IT SHOULD BE

Built for people who take their music seriously.

SYNCED KARAOKE
Real-time synchronized lyrics that auto-scroll. Karaoke mode highlights individual words as they're sung — line by line, syllable by syllable.
Lyrics appear above controls in gesture mode — frosted glass, always accessible.
LOFI MODE
Instantly transform any track — slowed speed (0.9x), pitch correction, and native reverb effects. Your chill, your way, one tap.
GESTURE PLAYER
Immersive full-screen gesture controls. Swipe to skip tracks, swipe up/down for volume, double-tap to play/pause. Album art fills your screen.
AUTO-QUEUE
Automatically queues recommended songs for endless playback. Smart Library with Favorites, History, custom Playlists, and channel subscriptions.
PRIVACY FIRST
No tracking, no ads, no data collection. All favorites, playlists, and history stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.
Account login is optional — never required.
OFFLINE DOWNLOADS
Download songs and videos for offline listening. Take your library anywhere, even without a connection.
DYNAMIC THEMING
UI adapts colors from the currently playing album art. Glassmorphic elements, smooth transitions, and a lush dark aesthetic throughout.
AUDIO QUALITY
Choose High, Medium, or Low quality. Platform-specific native audio effects. Multi-language audio auto-detection and switching.
INSTANT LAUNCH
Zero-wait startup with parallel background loading. RapidAPI fallback ensures playback never stops if the primary API hiccups.
