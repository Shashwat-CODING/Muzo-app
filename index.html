Features

Everything it should be

Built for people who take their music seriously, with no compromises.

Synced Karaoke Lyrics
Real-time synchronized lyrics that auto-scroll as the song plays. Karaoke mode highlights individual words syllable-by-syllable — the most immersive way to follow along.
Lofi Mode
Instantly transform any track with slowed speed, pitch correction, and native reverb effects. One tap to turn any song into something you'll melt into.
Privacy First
No tracking, no ads, zero data collection. Favorites, playlists, and history are stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your phone — ever.
Offline Downloads
Download songs and entire playlists for offline listening. Take your library anywhere, whether you're on a plane or underground — no connection required.
Gesture Player
Immersive full-screen gesture controls — swipe left/right to skip tracks, swipe up/down for volume, double-tap to play/pause. No UI clutter.
Smart Auto-Queue
Automatically queues recommended songs for endless playback. Smart Library keeps Favorites, History, custom Playlists, and channel subscriptions all in sync.
Dynamic Theming
The UI extracts colors from your current album art and rebuilds itself around them. Glassmorphic elements, smooth transitions, and a rich ambient aesthetic throughout.
Lossless Audio Quality
Choose High, Medium, or Low quality. Platform-native audio effects. Multi-language audio detection and automatic switching between audio sources.
Instant Launch
Zero-wait startup with parallel background loading. RapidAPI fallback ensures playback never stops even if the primary API hiccups or goes offline.
